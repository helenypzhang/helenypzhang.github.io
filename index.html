Yupei Zhang

I am a full-time research assistant in the Department of Pathology, School of Clinical Medicine at the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Maximus Yeung . Before that, I was a full-time research assistant in the Centre for Intelligent Multidimensional Data Analysis Limited, InnoHK, CityU & Oxford.

My research interests is Medical Image Analysis, Multi-modal Diagnosis and Vision-and-Language Pretraining.

I received my MSc degree in Multimedia Information Technology from the City University of Hong Kong (CityU). Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Digital Media Technology from Beijing Language and Culture University (BLCU), China.
